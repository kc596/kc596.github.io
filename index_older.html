Deparador

NIT Silchar fraternity feels ecstatic to bring you the premier debating competition of North East Indiaand the flag bearer event for the literary society at NIT Silchar. Thus to keep up with the richintellectual spirit and high expectations of the avant-garde citizens who have been patiently waitingyearlong for this pinnacle of deliberation we have come up with 3 grilling rounds to find out whoshall be the erudite champion they are as follow:

*Participants from outside NIT Silchar shall have their prelims a day before the finals

Pre Deprador Round 1

It is the preliminary screening round for all the registered participants it will require the participants togive their views (3 minutes max) on a topic that would be notified to them 24 hours before theprelims and they would have to answer a question on the topic from the judges (90 sec max). Theparticipants shall be judged on basis of their point of view and their answer. Depending on thenumber of participants the fudges shall decide the number of participants going through to the nextrounds.

Pre Deprador Round 2

This round shall be divided into two parts
Part 1:
This will require the selected participants to submit a video of themselves giving their views on atopic selected by the judges (to be disclosed at the end of round 1) the video should not be longerthan 3 minutes. the judges shall select the best ones to be called for part 2.
Part 2:
The participants called from part 1 will engage in a fully-fledged debate (topics and rules to benotified 24 hours before debate). The judges shall select top 10 participants to compete in the finalevent.

Depardor Finals

The final debate shall be held on 12 Feb will be moderated and judged by special guests who shallnotify the rules and agenda 7 days before the finals
*Prelims for participants from outside NIT
They shall have to debate on topics given by judges on the spot with 2 min time for thought processand 3 min speaking time and 3 minutes to answer to the questions.

Director's Cut

"Director's Cut" is a module in the annual cultural fest of National Institute of Technology, Silchar called Incandescence. One of the most prestigious events to take place under Dramatics and Dance Club, NIT Silchar, Director's Cut is an online short film submission competition. The entries that are submitted are then screened in the campus and are judged by eminent personalities. Over the years, Director's Cut has been successful in igniting the interest of direction, cinematography and acting in many college students. A testimony of that is the sheer quality of films that have been produced for Director's Cut. The Chain, Breathe, Cross Connection and many such short gems can be found on the official YouTube channel. Out of the many short movies, "The River Island Tale" directed by Porag Jyoti Gogoi and Hritiz Gogoi has garnered national recognition as it has been to various film festivals of India such as Brahmaputra Film Festival and The Woodpecker International Film Festival. This year too, Director's Cut is returning in Incandescence 2016 and the quality of films that are to be submitted is definitely something to look forward to:

Indie Lake

Very few times an event captures the imagination and soul of the public on such a large scale. What INDIE LAKE 2015 was able to achieve was nothing short of magical. When a group of young college students driven by hope, foresight and sheer unparalleled ambition come together to organize an event such as INDIE LAKE, success is inevitable. The boat rides, the lake views, the setting sun and its horizon are all set to return as we prepare to welcome amongst our midst INDIE LAKE 2.0 this February. So get ready to sing along or even better grab a guitar and join in as the stage is set and INDIE LAKE 2.0 promises two spectacular evenings by the lake which shall be etched in your memory forever.

Indie Lake being held in the annual festival of Incandescence is held during a duration of 2 days. The first day is dedicated to VOICE OF INCAND which is a solo singing competition and is judged purely on the basis of singing talent although they may be accompanied by an instrument of their choice.
The second day of Indie Lake is dedicated to INDIE LAKE UNPLUGGED which is a purely acoustic event for the bands to showcase their talent. Bands are allowed to play any acoustic instrument be it the guitar strings or the dhol or the precaution instruments.

For more info, visit indielake.in

Voice Of Incand

Taking place under the banner of Indie Lake, Voice of Incand is the annual solo vocals competition of NIT Silchar. It took place for the first time in 2015 and was a resounding success with many contestants extending forward their participation. The 2015 edition of Voice of Incand was judged by Barry Kharnior and Andrew Mailot. Sunep Oz was declared the winner of Voice of Incand while Arantxa Wanchi Simsang was declared the runners up.

Indie Lake Unplugged

Indie Lake Unplugged is one of the events of the annual acoustic competition of NIT Silchar known as Indie Lake. It is the acoustic band competition where many bands from all over the nation contest in order to attain the coveted title and cash prizes worth 30K INR. Although it started only in 2015, Indie Lake Unplugged has started to gain wide popularity among the bands. The first version of Indie Lake Unplugged was judged by Barry Kharnaior and Andrew Mawlot. Fuzz Town were declared the winners of Indie Lake Unplugged and Children of Hope were adjudged the runners up.

Rules and Regulations

  1. The contestants may use one instrument, if at all required.
  2. In case of a karaoke track, contestants are required to bring their own tracks.
  3. There is no restriction in the choice of genre or language.
  4. Each participant is allowed to sing only one song for a duration of maximum 5 minutes.
  5. Only 5 contestants will qualify for the final round.
  6. The contestants are required to provide links of at least one demo audio songs link or a video link of their performance.
  7. The decision of the judges will be final and irrevocable.

Indie Lake Unplugged

Register

Rules and Regulations

  1. Only acoustic bands are eligible to participate in the event.
  2. Participants are requested to perform in groups of two or a maximum of four.
  3. Any kind of acoustic instrument is allowed, however contestants are allowed to use electric bass guitars and pianos/keyboard.
  4. Participants are allowed to perform only one song during the prelims.
  5. The participants which will be selected to perform in the final round are allowed to perform two songs or musical pieces.
  6. Only 5 bands/participants shall qualify for the final round.
  7. Contestants are required to bring their own musical instruments.
  8. The bands are required to provide links of at least one demo audio songs link or a video link of their performance.
  9. The decision of the judges is final and irrevocable.
